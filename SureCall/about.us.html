About Us

Founded in 2001 and based in the Silicon Valley, SureCall is the cell phone signal booster leader in combining high quality technology with innovative design. SureCall produces award-winning boosters that dramatically improve cell phone reception for homes, cars and businesses. With customers including Chrysler, Hewlett-Packard and ExxonMobil, as well as other major institutions, such as Stanford, Duke and Wake Forest Universities, SureCall continues to develop industry-leading boosters that address the changing needs of all types of cellular and mobile broadband users. Awards include the 2014 and 2015 Commercial Integrator BEST Award, the 2014 and 2015 EXC!TE Award and the 2015 Best Technology Integrator Award. These awards recognize the impact SureCall products have on the industry in terms of the installers' productivity, the dealers' profitability, the clients' satisfaction and the industry's vitality. SureCall's line of FCC-approved boosters for personal, commercial and industrial use, increase the range and reception of almost any cellular and/or data transmission.

How it Work

Signals sent from a cell tower are received by an outside antenna, amplified by the booster and then transmitted to a cellphone via an inside antenna. When a call is made, the signal is sent to the inside antenna amplified and sent back to the outside antenna.

Why SureCall

While most companies use words like "quality" and "innovation" to describe their products SureCall has repeatedly proven that claim through combining high quality technology with innovative design – and passing those qualities on to consumers and end-users, whether it’s for mobile, home, in-building, M2M (machine-to-machine) or direct connect cell phone booster applications.

There are several distinct reasons, which make our cellular signal boosters better than the competition including:

Separate uplink/downlink lanes
Shared lanes cause signal collisions resulting in dropped calls, poor voice/data quality and result in fewer simultaneous users.
First to market innovation
SureCall’s Enterprise signal boosters are built using compartmentalized design. This proven method reduces oscillation, or noise, to the cellular towers and it provides the power to support simultaneous calls being made or received.
LTE/4G
SureCall was the first booster company to develop 4G cell phone signal boosters for AT&T, Verizon T-Mobile and Sprint.
Highly linear cellular signal boosters
SureCall carries the only line of highly linear cellular signal boosters. Linearity allows for more simultaneous users and larger areas of coverage.
The outer casing
Our Enterprise and Custom Installed SureCall cellular signal boosters are built with metal shells surrounding their inner workings. Metal is much better at dissipating heat, an important element in cell phone booster functionality.
Adjustable Gain
Another example of SureCall's first-to-market innovation is adjustable dB gain. Because cellular technology is not as stable as cable technology, there are a variety of factors that can affect cellular and data reception quality. Having the ability to adjust dB gain is just another way SureCall ensures that its products and their users will receive the best cellular reception on the market.
All carrier cell phone signal boosters
SureCall was the first to develop boosters that enhance frequencies for all 2G, 3G and 4G Verizon, AT&T, T-Mobile and Sprint service. This puts a cost-effective, all-in-one solution in the hands of consumers and end-users.
