Home Booster Solution: Preferred Property Inspection

Dilemma: Increasing cellphone reception for a home office residing in a canyon.

Solution: SureCall's FlexPro Cellular Signal Booster for Home/Office

Key Benefits

• Easily receive and make business calls without having them dropped

• Increased cellphone and data reception throughout the home for the entire family

Customer background

Bill Crook has owned and operated Preferred Property Inspection, a real estate inspection business on the San Francisco Peninsula, since 1992. A vital part of the real estate transaction process, Crook has gained a reputation as one of the premier home inspectors for nearly two decades. Also a licensed general contractor, Crook has significantly remodeled two of his own homes while remodeling many others as a partner in a previous home remodeling business.

http://www.preferredpropertyinspection.net/

The Challenge

Operating his business out of his own home was difficult because Crook lives in a canyon in San Carlos, CA. In the two decades he's lived there he’s never been able to take or make cellular calls to his real estate clients—a distinct disadvantage for a business where the majority of dialogue is done with his cellphone, on the road and in his home office. In the past, when he made a call regarding an inspection or a report he was preparing, he had to step outside his home on the front lawn. But this did not allow him to reference home inspections for ongoing jobs on his office PC. He would either have to print up the full report to discuss it with his client or call them back with more information when he’d referenced it.

Crook heard about SureCall boosters from a neighbor who owned one and swore by the great cellular reception he was receiving. After researching various manufacturers on his own, he chose a SureCall cell phone booster because of the quality metal design and accessories compared to its competitors. He also liked the fact that they supported a three-year warranty, should anything happen.

Results

Installed over a weekend, Crook now enjoys clear cellular reception in his home office where he had none before. Conversations with real estate agent clients are no longer a hassle. With a FlexPro adjustable cellular booster, his whole family can also take advantage of the enhanced reception they receive on their own cell phones.
